Getting There & Home

Pearl Farm is an island — the only way on and off is the resort ferry. Miss your boat and you wait for the next one, so please get to the port early.

Check-In & Out

Check-in (Sat): on arrival, both ferries. Rooms are pre-assigned — grab your Activity Guide and Photo Hunt list at the desk.
Check-out (Sun): by 11:00 AM. Bring bags down before the last ferry.

Meals

Sat lunch: on arrival (early ferry).
Sat merienda: 4:30 PM, Maranao Restaurant.
Sat dinner: 7:00 PM, with the program.
Sun breakfast: 6:30–10:00 AM, open seating.
Sun lunch: from 11:00 AM, before you head out. Only one lunch is included per guest — this is for those who didn’t have Saturday’s lunch.
